About Me

My name is Tiwatope Obasa. I have always been passionate about turning ideas into wearable art and over the years I have honed skills in both design and garment construction. I focus on creating practical and stylish clothing for women in urban environments.

My interests include exploring new trends, sustainable fashion, and empowering women through clothing. With a commitment to quality and versatility, I aim to celebrate individuality through my designs.
